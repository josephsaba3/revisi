## Weak CTA Phrases

- learn more
- get started
- submit
- click here
- contact us
- discover more
- explore
- find out more

These are weak when used alone. They can work when paired with a specific promise, such as `Book a 15-minute teardown` or `See the sample report`.

## Pattern Flags

- Repeated sentence openings across adjacent sections.
- Three-item benefit lists where every item is abstract.
- Claims with no noun explaining the product or service.
- Multiple em dashes in short passages.
- Paragraphs longer than 45 words on a landing page.
- CTAs that do not state the action or expected result.
- Testimonials or proof claims without attribution or context.
